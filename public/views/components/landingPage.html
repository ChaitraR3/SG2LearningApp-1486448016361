Telefonica UK - O2 Companion

Telefonica UK Limited (trading as O2) is a telecommunications services provider in the United Kingdom owned by the Spanish multinational Telefonica. It is the second-largest mobile telecommunications provider in the United Kingdom and is headquartered in Slough, UK. Motto of O2 UK is More For You (Formally "Be More Dog"). Telefonica O2 UK Ltd. operates as a cellular communications network operator. The Company provides mobile and broadband services, as well as offers non-voice services, including text, media messaging, games, music and video, and data connections.
